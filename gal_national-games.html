National Games 2015

The National Games held in Feb 2015 at Thiruvanthapuram is the brightest feather in the cap of Kathakali School. We have participated in and coordinated a number of Mega-events in many nations - but this has by far been our most important project.

Both the opening and the closing ceremony were very successful. Our display of the various performing art forms of India was unparalleled in that we had over 5000 performers and a great number of back up crew. Kathakali School feels proud to say that the event went without any major hitches.
